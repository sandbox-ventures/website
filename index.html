How we build

Six principles guide every product we build and every team we assemble.

Portfolio approach

100 horses instead of one unicorn. We build a portfolio of practical SaaS products — multiple sustainable successes rather than a single high-risk moonshot.

Practicality first

Solutions for genuine market needs. No trend-chasing, no building for hypothetical futures.

Lean execution

Maximum output from minimal resources. Every hour and every dollar is spent with intention.

Speed to market

Ship fast, validate quickly, iterate based on real usage. Delay is the enemy of learning.

Profitable from day one

Sound economics from the start. Revenue, not fundraising rounds, is the measure of progress.

Small teams

Skilled individuals who move fast and make decisions. No layers, no committees, no waiting.

Who we are

Sandbox.Ventures was founded by experienced entrepreneurs from Clearhaus, a leading European payment company they previously built and scaled.

The team has deep experience in payments, software development, and business operations. We've built products, grown teams, and solved real problems for real customers.

Build with us

Have a validated SaaS idea and need a team to build it? We partner with founders who have domain expertise and a clear market. We bring engineering, capital, and operational experience.

We optimize for early revenue, not fundraising rounds. If that sounds like your kind of partnership, reach out.
