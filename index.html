About Us

Who We Are

Our Team

Sandbox.Ventures was founded by a team of experienced entrepreneurs with backgrounds in fintech and technology. The founding team members were all part of Clearhaus, a leading European payment company that several of them previously founded and successfully scaled.

Our team has built products, grown tech teams, and solved real problems for real customers. With expertise in payments, coding, and business, we identify opportunities and execute efficiently in today's market.

Our Vision

Sandbox.Ventures moves away from pursuing unicorns at all costs. Instead, we build a portfolio of profitable, sustainable SaaS businesses addressing genuine market needs.

We create valuable technology through lean execution and sustainable business models. Our focus on early profitability builds resilient businesses that deliver long-term value. Our "100 horses instead of unicorns" philosophy prioritizes multiple practical successes over high-risk moonshots.
